Hello! My name is Michael Maze.

Over the course of my adult life I went from soldier in the United States Army, to handy-man, to detention officer eventually finding myself in college studying, of all things, networking and communications management. This education opened some doors into the telecom world, which in turn led to a position in data analytics, process documentation, and product ownership. Since, I have also obtained my MBA, my Master of Global Management, and I am currently working on my Maser of Business Analytics.

Some hobbies that aren't listed are that I like to read anything and (just about) everything, especially books in the Star Wars universe. I am a HUGE Star Wars nerd. Darth Bane was my favorite series, and don't get me started on who I would have had train Anakin instead of Obi-Wan. What a mistake.

Jiu Jitsu is another passion of mine. I truly believe in the powers of the martial art. Not only in its ability to improve physical fitness, but also in its ability to improve mental health and discipline.

I also enjoy getting outside on my adventure motorcycle and seeing what the open desert has to offer. I am an Arizonan born and raised, and want to try to see as much of the beautiful desert landscape as I can.

From a work standpoint, I always want to dig down deeper. I feel that there is always something left to glean from a given dataset. Understanding that, I like to take a tiered approach to every project. First, determine the problem statement. What are we trying to solve for? Second, look at the data available and determine what is relevant, and what is not. Third, compile the data in a structured analytical database and start to dig. I isnt uncommon for me to dream about complex problems and solve them that way. Ultimately, I get intrinsic satisfaction from solving complex puzzles that turn into successful recommendations. Money is nice, too.
